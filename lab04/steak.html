Pro tip: If you are cooking hot and fast, then you shouls the steak out 10 degrees farenheit before your desired doneness. For example, if you want medium rare(135 farenheit), then pull the steak off at 125 degrees farenheit. With this thing called carry-over cooking due to high temperature cooking, the steak will rise 5 to 10 degrees after you pull it off the pan.
